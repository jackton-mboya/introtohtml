Spring Cleaning

First goes floordust, then newspapers stacked near the bed. Peanut shells swept out of hiding between mattress and rug. Toenails clipped. Sprouts of a beard shaved off. With hourly glasses of Deer Park Water and the barest of food, the body sheds winter fat and filler.

  • You are someone new. I will be someone new, like you, and promise not to hear the rattle our bones make moving from empty closets and all through the room.
